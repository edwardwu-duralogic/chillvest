How It Works & Key Specifications

How It Works

1

Pre-Cool

Place ColdBank cells in a standard freezer or ice cooler for 1–2 hours before the shift.

2

Wear

Put on the vest. Gel cells sit against the body, separated by a thin comfort fabric layer.

3

Sustained Cooling

Body heat conducts into the cold gel, which absorbs it evenly — no cold spike, steady release over hours.

4

Swap, Don't Stop

When a ColdBank warms up, swap in a fresh one in seconds — all-day cooling with no mid-shift freezer trips.

5

Fluid Circulation

USB-powered DC 5V motor circulates coolant fluid through the vest, ensuring even temperature distribution across all cooling zones.
